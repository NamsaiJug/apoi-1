About the index

AAPO assesses how openly national parliaments across Asia-Pacific operate, so citizens, media, and reformers can see exactly where each parliament stands. Every parliament is scored against the same set of questions, organized into three dimensions of openness.

Asian Alliance for Parliamentary Openness (AAPO)

AAPO Logo

We are a regional network of civil society organizations committed to parliamentary monitoring and democratic advocacy across the Asia-Pacific. Together, we have launched the Asia Parliamentary Openness Index, a collaborative initiative designed to assess and promote transparency within parliaments throughout the region.
