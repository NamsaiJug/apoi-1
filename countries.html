About the Index Methodology
Raw Score
The raw score is the sum of the points earned across all assessment questions, with each question worth one point. The total possible score may vary between countries because some assessment questions are not applicable in certain national contexts. These questions are excluded from the calculation, so each country's score is based only on the questions that apply to its parliament.
Overall Score
The overall score is calculated by converting the raw score into a percentage using the following formula: Overall Score = (Raw Score ÷ Total Possible Score) × 100

To ensure fair comparisons across countries with different parliamentary systems, the scores of bicameral legislatures are calculated by averaging the scores of their lower and upper chambers. For unicameral legislatures, the score of the single chamber is used.
